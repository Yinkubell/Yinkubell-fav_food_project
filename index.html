FRIED RICE

The Origin of Fried rice

Fried rice first developed during the Sui Dynasty in China and as such all fried rice dishes can trace their origins to chinese fried rice.

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables,seafood,or meat.

Ingredients Measurements
Boiled basmati rice 500gm
Chopped spring onions 1 bunch
Chopped green pepper half cup
Chopped carrot half cup
Garlic paste half teaspoon
Ginger paste half teaspoon
Powdered black pepper half teaspoon
Light soya sauce 2 table spoon
Vinegar 1 tablespoon
Green chilli sauce 1 table spoon
Tomato ketchup 2 table spoon
Sunflower oil half cup
Salt 4 pinches

Steps to prepare fried rice

  1. Boil the rice or steam cook them
  2. Saute or the veggies
  3. mix sauces in the veggies and cook with rice for 3-5 minutes
  4. Garnish and serve
